About Me



Hey, I’m Ashley Campusano and I used to work as a Child Development Specialist.

You’re probably wondering why that matters.

Well, when you’ve worked with toddlers, you become acutely aware of their attention span (or lack thereof). It turns out the average attention span of a consumer is just as short – about 8 seconds.

That means you have 8 seconds to get a prospect to choose you out of the other 5,000 ads they see every day.

So how can I make that happen?

Combine an understanding of CRM, SEO, SEM, and UX/UI, with a natural ability to connect with others through empathy, compassion, and ingenuity, and you’ve got yourself a skillful and diverse copywriter!
